About Us

We have been catering in Utah since 2006 and have catered many events, ranging from 10 guests to 800 guests! We have been honored to catered not only wedding receptions, birthdays and company holiday parties, but also movie and commercial shoots, fundraising events, open houses, corporate catering and more!

Customers Matter

Current and previous clients will first say that we are "easy to work with" and that's a true statement! We makes things simple for you from booking, to payment, to the event. We will adjust menus to fit your need, often with no added costs!

Great Eats

As you browse our menus, please keep in mind these are our most popular menu bookings. We will gladly work with any budget to accommodate your needs by changing menus up and creating new menus just for you!

Booking Requirements

  • Our menus and pricing are based on a minimum guest count of 50 persons. If you are interested in catering for a lower guest count, please contact for additional pricing.
  • First time clients, we will require a non-refundable booking deposit along with signed contract. Full balance will be due as outlined on contract.
  • Sales Tax of 7.85% and gratuity of 18% will be added to final total. If you are tax exempt, we will require your tax exempt ID. Repeat clients gratuity is 15%.
  • We accept all forms of payment: Check, Cash, Credit Cards, Venmo, Paypal
